Internet Connected Powerbar

using Electric Imp

This project takes a typical powerbar and connects it to the internet. Instead of all plugs being on at one time, you can turn each outlet on and off individually from your mobile app.

Hardware Used: Electric Imp + April Board, 8 Channel Relay Board, 8 Outlet Powerbar. You should have previous experience with low voltage devices controlling high voltage. **** Be careful when working with high voltage currents like that of your wall outlet, it can be VERY dangerous.

This is meant to be a trainer/starter project, and lays out the basic framework for a more advanced app/api.
